Site Plan

Site Name: Dust Busters

The name represents a professional cleaning company dedicated to maintaining clean and hygienic spaces for homes and businesses.

Optional domain availability: dustbusterscleaning.com

Site Purpose

The site provides information on professional cleaning services, including residential and commercial cleaning, along with a booking system for appointments.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What cleaning packages do you offer for residential properties?

Scenario 2: Where can I find contact information for customer inquiries?

Color Schema

I will use a Midnight-Green colour for the header and footer, Black for the navigation and Tiffany-Blue for my headings background.

These colors are relavant to Dust Busters as they align with logo and uniform colors. They are also friendly to the visually impaired users as they as not so shouting and blend in well with the browser default white or black font colors.


The codes to my chosen colours are as follows:

Midnight-Green: #005f73ff,

Tiffany-Blue: #94d2bdff

White & Black: #fff & , #001219ff for

Headers Body Text 1 Text 2
[#005f73ff] [#94d2bdff] [#001219ff] [#fff]

Typography

I am going to use two fonts "Raleway" and "Nunito"


I will use Nunito for the headings and Raleway for all my paragraph text.
